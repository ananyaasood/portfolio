I — Philosophy
Luxury is perception shaped through intention and narrative — not price, not logo, not mass appeal.

I don't believe in creating for the masses. Every project I touch — whether it's a garment, a visual system, or a piece of software — is built with a specific audience in mind: people who pay attention, who value depth over trend, authenticity over algorithm.

My approach to creation is rooted in restraint. Scarcity isn't a marketing tactic; it's a design principle. When something is intentionally limited, it demands more from both creator and consumer. It forces you to care.

I treat every medium as a canvas for exploring identity. Garments are reflections of inner states. Interfaces are architecture for perception. Film is frozen time, coded with meaning. The medium changes. The philosophy doesn't.

II — Background
01

Fashion & Brand

Founder and Creative Director of RFLXNS (Reflections-Luxe) — a high-end independent streetwear label positioned beyond mass culture. Built brand identity, visual language, collection concepts, and physical launch presence from zero. Culture-first. Scarcity-driven.

02

Film & Visual

End-to-end visual direction — from concept to final cut. Streetwear campaigns, product videography, short-form brand films, editorial reels. Every frame is intentional. Motion pacing, music sync, and cinematic storytelling built around brand narrative.

03

Systems & Technology

Self-hosted AI architectures on Apple Silicon. Multi-API automation pipelines. Personal pentesting environments. Reproducible development setups. Building private, local-first intelligence systems that don't depend on anyone else's cloud.
